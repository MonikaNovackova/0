Experienced data scientist, statistician and R programmer with a demonstrated history of working in the higher education industry. My main professional interest include data analysis, machine learning, statistics and econometric modelling. I am also interested in Climate Change Economics and Sustainability. In addition, I enjoy coding in R, Python and web-design.

In the present, I work as a Research Fellow in Sustainability at the department of Geography, University of Sussex. My current research is focused on estimating of effects of droughts in sub-Saharan Africa using economic and econometrics methods. My work will contribute to the Sussex Sustainability Research Programme.
